NCT DREAM

25 August 2016

NCT DREAM is NCT’s sub-group comprising seven members
– MARK, RENJUN, JENO, HAECHAN, JAEMIN, CHENLE and JISUNG.
The once “teenage team” now grew up as one of the iconic K-POP boy bands.
Releasing their first debut song “Chewing Gum” on August 25, 2016,
the group has released numerous hits including
“ISTJ,” “Glitch Mode,”“Hot Sauce,”“Beatbox,”“Hello Future,”,“We Go Up,” and “My First and Last” etc.

SEVENTEEN

26 May 2015

Seventeen is a South Korean boy band formed by Pledis Entertainment.
The group consists of thirteen members: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi,
DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino.
Seventeen has conquered a large audience since its debut and has grown into an internationally
recognized K-Pop group with signature music and performances.
